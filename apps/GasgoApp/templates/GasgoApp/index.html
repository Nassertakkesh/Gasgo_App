How it Works?

Gasoline Delivered.
Anytime. Anywhere. Anyone.

  • Step 1

    Sign up!

    During this phase, you tell us all the important information about you and your car. We require your license plate number, car make, model, year, and color. We do this to be able to identify your car when we are filling it up, and to understand the dimensions of the tank and how much gas is needed for a full tank.

  • Step 2

    Request a Fill

    When your car is low on gas, you simply request a fill, drop a pin of here your car is located and leave your gas flap open. That's it. Its that simple. (you can request any fill amount greater than one gallon).

  • Step 3

    Request Accepted & Car is Filled

    A driver then accepts the request, goes to the nearest gas station to you and fills up the amount of gas you requested. Then, he drives to your car, fills up the amountyou requested, and closes the flap.

  • Step 4

    Finally, You are Charged!

    Once the verication is completed and we verify that the driver did in fact fill up your car with the amount requested, then we charge you!
